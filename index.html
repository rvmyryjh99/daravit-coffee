About Daravit Coffee

Daravit Coffee is a premier coffee company based in the heart of Kampala, Uganda. We are passionate about bringing you the finest coffee beans sourced directly from local farmers, ensuring quality and sustainability in every cup.

Our mission is to share the rich coffee culture of Uganda with the world while supporting our local communities. From carefully selected beans to expertly crafted blends, we take pride in every step of our process.
